FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use Chess Attendance & Billing System? ▾
None at all. The day-to-day admin dashboard is a web interface — like using any website. We handle the initial deployment. After that, adding students, marking attendance, and pulling reports requires zero technical knowledge.
Does it work for online chess coaching? ▾
Absolutely. You can create batches in ONLINE mode. Coaches mark attendance after each session from their phone or laptop. Billing and reporting work identically for online and offline batches.
Can multiple coaches use it simultaneously? ▾
Yes. Each coach gets their own login and only sees their assigned batches. The admin sees everything across all coaches, batches, and branches from one login.
Where is the data stored? Is it secure? ▾
On your own server (VPS). We don't have access to your student data after deployment. All passwords are hashed. The application runs over HTTPS. Your data is never on our infrastructure.
Can I import my existing student data? ▾
On the Enterprise plan we include a full data import from Google Sheets. For Starter and Professional, adding students manually takes 2–3 minutes each, or we can quote a one-time migration service.
